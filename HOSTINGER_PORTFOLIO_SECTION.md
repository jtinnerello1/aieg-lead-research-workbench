## Section title

AIEG Lead Research Workbench

## Short description

This is a synthetic portfolio demo showing how AI can support human-in-the-loop lead research, qualification, scoring, and CRM-ready preparation.

The application was created as a Hermes-assisted workflow demo. Hermes was used as part of the design and build-orchestration process to help define the product concept, structure the lead qualification workflow, generate synthetic scenarios, shape the scoring logic, validate safety boundaries, and support QA planning.

The app itself does not run live prospect research. It does not scrape websites, use real company data, contact real people, update a CRM, or send outreach messages.

All companies, contacts, signals, scores, summaries, and CRM records shown in this demo are fictitious and created for demonstration purposes only.

The purpose of the demo is to show how AI can help a human team review leads faster, prioritize better opportunities, flag uncertainty, and prepare cleaner CRM records without removing human judgment from the process.

## How to use this demo

1. Start with the ICP setup panel to see the target customer profile and scoring criteria.
2. Open the synthetic lead list to review fictional companies created for the demo.
3. Select a lead to view the AI-style research summary, qualification score, confidence level, and risk flags.
4. Use the human review queue to see how a person reviews, approves, holds, or disqualifies a lead before any CRM action.
5. Open the CRM export preview to see what would be prepared for a CRM record. This is a preview only. No CRM is connected.
6. Review the traceability section to see what information was used, what was inferred, and what requires human review.
