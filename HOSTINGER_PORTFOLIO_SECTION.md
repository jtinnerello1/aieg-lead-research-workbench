## Section title

AIEG Prospect Research Workbench

## Short description

This is a synthetic portfolio demo showing how AI-assisted research can help a human team turn fictitious prospect information into a clear research brief for review.

The application was created as a Hermes-assisted workflow demo. Hermes was used as part of the design and build-orchestration process to help define the product concept, structure the prospect research workflow, generate synthetic scenarios, shape the research brief, validate safety boundaries, and support QA planning.

The app itself does not run live prospect research. It does not scrape websites, use real company data, contact real people, connect to external systems, or send outreach messages.

All companies, contacts, signals, scores, summaries, and research briefs shown in this demo are fictitious and created for demonstration purposes only.

The purpose of the demo is to show how AI can help a human team review prospects faster, prioritize better opportunities, flag uncertainty, and prepare cleaner discovery conversations without removing human judgment from the process.

## How to use this demo

1. Start with the ICP setup panel to see the target customer profile and scoring criteria.
2. Use search and filters to narrow the fictitious prospect list.
3. Select a prospect to view the AI-style research summary, fit score, confidence level, and risk flags.
4. Click “Generate Research Brief” to reveal the simulated research packet.
5. Use the human review panel to change review status and add reviewer notes. These are saved locally in the browser.
6. Review the traceability section to see what information was used, what was inferred, and what requires human review.

## In-app / page disclosure
